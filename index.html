I want to learn Irish, but how?

Learning a language requires daily commitment. But what to study each day and how to make it interesting?

Gaeilge Galore will teach you!

Gaeilge Galore sends you an email every morning covering a new topic, relevant to your life. We create fun, engaging content, so you can just learn, without planning and without stress.

What will I learn?

Every day, we will discuss a new topic, and you will learn:

  • 5 new words.
  • 1 seanfhocail.
  • 1 simple grammar rule.

Additionally, you will receive:

  • Audio conversation questions, so you can practice speaking as Gaeilge.
  • Suggested phrases for these conversations, using your new words.
  • An Irish article to read, and translation of the most important words.
  • An Irish radio show or podcast to listen to.

About Gaeilge Galore

Gaeilge Galore was set up by Áine during lockdown 2020. Inspired to get her Irish back to Leaving Cert standard, Áine created a plan and began learning.

But it was hard gathering new vocab and thinking of new topics to learn about. Besides, who could Áine speak to as Gaeilge? How could she practice speaking Irish?

Áine realised all her planning of what to learn could help others just focus on learning. That's how Gaeilge Galore was born: Gaeilge Galore plans what you should learn so you can focus on learning Irish. It's efficient, fun and relevant.
